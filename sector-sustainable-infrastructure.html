Sector: Sustainable Infrastructure

Infrastructure built to last decades.

EV charging, solar canopies, LED conversions, and energy systems installed by a contractor who understands the operational environment they're going into.

Who We Serve

Sustainability commitments require a contractor who can actually deliver.

National retailers and commercial property owners are under real pressure to decarbonize their portfolios. The goal is clear. The execution is hard. Sustainable infrastructure projects (EV charging arrays, solar canopy installations, building-wide LED conversions) require a contractor who can manage civil, electrical, and structural scope at the same time, across multiple sites, without missing store operations.

RJB has been executing sustainable infrastructure programs for national retail clients for years. We've installed EV charging stations across large parking lots at operating stores, erected solar canopies over active drive-throughs, and converted entire commercial buildings to LED without a single hour of planned store downtime.

We coordinate with utilities, equipment vendors, and energy program managers so the client doesn't have to manage five separate contractors. One team. One point of accountability. Done right the first time.

What We Install

End-to-end sustainable infrastructure.

01
EV Charging Infrastructure
Trenching, conduit, electrical rough-in, and EVSE installation for Level 2 and DC fast charger deployments at commercial and retail locations. We manage the full site-work-to-energization scope.
02
Solar Canopy Construction
Foundation, structural steel, and solar panel installation for parking lot canopy systems at active commercial sites. We phase the work to maintain parking operations throughout construction.
03
LED Lighting Conversions
Interior and exterior LED conversions for retail stores, warehouses, and parking lots. Full fixture replacement handled by our teams with minimal operational impact.
04
Energy Management Systems
Building controls, smart metering, and energy monitoring system installations that support ongoing energy reduction programs across retail and commercial portfolios.
05
National Portfolio Programs
Multi-site sustainable infrastructure programs managed from a single RJB project team. Consistent specifications, consistent timelines, and consolidated reporting for the client's sustainability team.
06
Utility & Permit Coordination
We manage the utility interconnection applications, permit submissions, and jurisdictional inspections that make sustainable infrastructure programs slow down when the contractor doesn't know the process.
What Makes This Work Hard

Challenges we've already solved.

Active Site Operations

EV and solar installations happen in parking lots and on rooftops, not in empty fields. We maintain safe traffic flow, customer access, and store operations throughout every phase of construction.

Utility Interconnection Delays

Utility approvals and interconnection agreements are the most common cause of delay in sustainable infrastructure projects. We start the process early and track it relentlessly so it doesn't become the client's problem.

Multi-Trade Coordination

A solar canopy project involves civil, structural, electrical, and roofing trades. We manage all of them under one contract and one schedule, not as four separate vendor relationships the client has to coordinate.

Portfolio Reporting

Sustainability teams need documentation, incentive support, and progress data across many sites. We build reporting into every program so the client's sustainability commitments have the construction data to back them up.

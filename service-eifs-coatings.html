EIFS & Coatings.

EIFS & Coatings

Protect the building. Protect the brand.

Coatings aren't a finishing touch for RJB. They're where we started. Ron Bacskai founded RJB Contracting in 1992 as a specialized commercial coatings company, and that expertise has been at the core of everything we've built since. It's still the discipline RJB knows best.

We deliver full EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System) installations and remediation programs for commercial properties across the country, from substrate evaluation and prep through mesh application, base coat, and finish coat, with full system warranties backed by the manufacturers we've worked alongside for decades.

Coatings and EIFS do more than keep a location looking sharp. A sound, sealed exterior keeps water out, adds insulation value, and protects the structure underneath, so the building stays sound and on-brand at the same time.

For national retail brands, brand-color compliance isn't optional. RJB's coatings programs ensure every location matches your specified palette: exterior finishes, interior paint, and accent colors executed to brand spec and documented with color verification at every site.

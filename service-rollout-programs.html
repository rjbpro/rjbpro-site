Rollout Programs

Consistent quality. Every location.

Running a construction program across dozens or hundreds of locations is a different discipline than building one great store. It requires systems, not just skill. RJB has been managing national rollout programs for America's largest retail and foodservice brands for decades, and we've built the infrastructure to prove it.

Single-point accountability isn't a slogan. It's how we're structured. One program manager. One contact. One team responsible for every location from kickoff to closeout. We build rollout calendars, manage procurement, coordinate regional subcontractors, and report consistently so you always know where your program stands.

Our national vendor network and in-house trade capabilities mean we're not dependent on local availability to hit your schedule. Whether you're opening 10 locations this quarter or 200, RJB has the bandwidth and the processes to deliver, without quality slipping as the numbers grow.

What's Included

Built for scale from day one.

01
Program Management
One team manages every location: scheduling, procurement, subcontractor coordination, and reporting through a single point of contact. You get visibility without the overhead of managing multiple GCs.
02
Scheduling & Sequencing
We build rollout calendars that sequence locations by region, trade availability, and your operational windows. Then we hit them. No surprises, no bottlenecks, no missed openings.
03
Quality Control
Site supervisors, photo documentation, and punch-list tracking at every location ensure brand standards are met from store one to store five hundred. Quality doesn't degrade as the program scales.
04
National Vendor Network
RJB's established relationships with national suppliers and regional subs mean your program won't stall waiting for materials or crews. We've built the relationships so your program doesn't have to.

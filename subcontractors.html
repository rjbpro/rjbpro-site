Join the Network

We partner with qualified subcontractors nationwide

RJB's ability to execute national programs across 48 states depends on a network of reliable, skilled subcontractors who share our commitment to quality and accountability. When a sub joins the RJB network, they're not just getting work. They're becoming part of a team that takes pride in doing things right.

We select our trade partners the same way we select our employees: carefully, based on track record, capability, and alignment with our standards. We expect clear communication, consistent quality, and the professionalism that national retail clients require. In return, you get steady work, fair terms, and a general contractor who treats you like a partner rather than a number on a bid list.

Requirements

Our standards

01
Licensed & Insured
All RJB subcontractors must hold current trade licenses and carry appropriate general liability and workers' compensation coverage. This is a baseline. We verify before every engagement.
02
Proven Track Record
We want to see your work before we put it in front of our clients. References from commercial construction projects and a demonstrated history of delivering to spec and on schedule are required.
03
Open-Store Experience
Working in live retail and industrial environments is a skill set, not just a work permit. We look for subcontractors who understand the discipline, safety requirements, and customer awareness that open-store construction demands.
04
National Availability
Our programs span multiple states and regions simultaneously. Subcontractors who can operate across a wide geography, or who are willing to travel, are especially valuable to our network.

Site Surveys

Know before you build.

The most expensive problems in construction are the ones nobody saw coming. RJB's pre-construction site survey programs are built around a simple principle: get trained eyes on every location before a scope is finalized, a budget is set, or a crew is scheduled. What you find in the field, not on a drawing, is what drives accurate estimates and achievable schedules.

Our survey teams document existing conditions across structure, finishes, MEP systems, and site, capturing measurements, photos, and field notes in a format your design and estimating teams can actually use. We compare what's in the field against your design intent and flag conflicts before they become change orders on a live job.

For multi-site rollout programs, pre-program surveys are the difference between a controlled deployment and a program that spins out of schedule by location three. RJB surveys locations in advance of mobilization to build site-specific scopes, so day one is never a surprise.

What's Included

The data your program depends on.

01
Condition Assessments
Trained field teams document existing conditions (structure, finishes, MEP, and site) before a scope is written or a budget is set. Delivered in a standardized format so your team can act on the findings immediately.
02
As-Built Documentation
We capture what's actually in the field, not what the old drawings say. Measurements, photos, and field notes delivered in a usable format that feeds directly into design and estimating workflows.
03
Scope Verification
Survey data is cross-referenced against your design intent so scope gaps and conflicts are caught early, when they're cheap to fix. No more discovering a discrepancy after the crew has already mobilized.
04
Pre-Program Audits
For multi-site programs, RJB surveys locations in advance of mobilization to build site-specific scopes and eliminate surprises on day one. The same rigor at location 200 as at location one.

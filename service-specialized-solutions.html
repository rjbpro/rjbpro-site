Specialized Solutions

Built around your operation.

RJB partners directly with clients and industry stakeholders to design and deliver construction programs that don't fit a standard template. Whether it's a unique scope, an untested format, or a program that requires building the playbook from scratch — RJB brings the field experience, trade depth, and operational flexibility to make it work.

Not every construction challenge has a precedent. When a client comes to us with a program that nobody has figured out before, we don't hand it to a project manager and hope for the best. We sit down with your team, understand the operational reality, and design a construction approach that actually fits the way you work — not a generic solution stretched to cover something it wasn't designed for.

We've launched programs in formats that didn't exist before, built construction playbooks for new store concepts, and solved operational puzzles that other contractors walked away from. If the scope is complex, unconventional, or still being defined — that's where RJB does some of its best work.

Our Approach

Custom programs, proven delivery.

01
Collaborative Planning
We start by listening. Understanding your operational constraints, brand standards, and program goals before a scope is written. The best construction programs are built on a clear picture of what you're actually trying to accomplish — not a template from the last job.
02
Program Design
RJB works alongside your team to build a construction program with the right trades, timeline, and accountability structure. We define the scope, sequence the work, identify the risks, and build the processes before a crew ever mobilizes.
03
Scalable Execution
Once the model is proven, we scale it — same quality, same standards, across as many locations as the program demands. RJB's national infrastructure means we can take a pilot program from one site to a full rollout without rebuilding the approach from scratch.

Commercial Construction

Built for your busiest day

Retail Construction

Open-store work, at portfolio scale

Retail construction doesn't happen on an empty job site. It happens in stores that are open for business, surrounded by customers, with brand standards that don't bend and timelines that don't move. RJB has been doing this work for over 30 years, and we've built the systems to execute it reliably, at scale, across any geography.

Whether you're refreshing a handful of locations or rolling out a brand update across hundreds of stores, RJB brings the same disciplined approach: detailed pre-construction planning, experienced crews who don't need to be babysat, and communication that keeps your operations team informed at every step.

Our retail construction practice spans remodels, national rollouts, second-generation build-outs, and phased programs designed around your operating calendar. We work nights and weekends so your stores stay open and your customers never notice the work was happening.

What We Deliver

End-to-end retail construction

01
Remodels & Refreshes
Full interior remodels and brand-standard refresh programs executed in live stores. We plan around your traffic patterns and operating hours so the work never disrupts your business, or your customers.
02
National Rollouts
Coordinated multi-location programs deployed across dozens or hundreds of stores simultaneously. RJB has the field organization, vendor network, and program management infrastructure to execute rollouts at any scale, on schedule.
03
Store Build-Outs
Second-generation build-outs from shell to finished interior, delivered by a single accountable contractor. Coordinating all core trades means faster schedules, fewer change orders, and one throat to grab if anything goes sideways.
04
Phased & Overnight Work
Not all retail construction can wait for a closure window. RJB specializes in phased construction and overnight work that keeps your store operational, your team safe, and your brand standards intact from day one.

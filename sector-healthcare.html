Sector: Healthcare

Precision construction. No margin for error.

Healthcare construction demands more from the contractor: tighter compliance, cleaner environments, and a crew that understands the sensitivity of every space they touch.

Who We Serve

Healthcare construction is a higher standard. We're ready for it.

The rapid expansion of retail health (clinics inside pharmacies, urgent care at strip centers, medical offices in high-traffic retail corridors) has created a construction challenge that sits at the intersection of retail speed and healthcare compliance. Most contractors can do one. Very few can do both.

RJB has built healthcare spaces inside and adjacent to operating retail environments. We understand the regulatory landscape, the infection control requirements, and the finish standards that healthcare brands demand. We also understand how to hit a retail-pace construction schedule without cutting corners that matter in a clinical setting.

We work with national pharmacy chains expanding their health clinic footprints, urgent care operators building out new locations, and healthcare systems looking for a contractor who can execute a multi-unit build program with consistency and accountability.

What Makes Healthcare Hard

Challenges we've already solved.

Regulatory Complexity

Healthcare construction is subject to local health department requirements, ADA standards, and infection control regulations on top of standard building codes. We navigate the full regulatory stack so the client doesn't have to.

Occupied Environments

Building inside or adjacent to an active clinical space means dust, noise, and vibration can't be an afterthought. ICRA barriers, negative air pressure zones, and after-hours scheduling are standard practice for our teams.

Finish Precision

Clinical environments demand higher finish quality than typical commercial construction. Seamless floors, tight drywall tolerances, and antimicrobial surface requirements are non-negotiable. Our teams are trained on the difference.

Certificate of Occupancy Timing

Healthcare facilities can't open without CO. We manage inspection sequences, documentation requirements, and punch list completion to ensure the CO timeline aligns with the brand's opening date, not the other way around.

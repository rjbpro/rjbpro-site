Life at RJB

Ambitious people. Real results.

At RJB, the work is real and so are the opportunities. We're a family-owned company, which means decisions get made by people who care about the long term — not a quarterly earnings report. If you're someone who shows up, solves problems, and takes pride in what you build, you'll find your place here.

From project managers running multi-state rollout programs to carpenters who take their craft seriously, the RJB team is made up of people who are genuinely good at what they do. We invest in training, promote from within, and build careers alongside buildings. When you join RJB, you're not just filling a position — you're becoming part of the infrastructure that keeps America's retail brands running.

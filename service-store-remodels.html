Store Remodels

Open tonight. Remodeled by morning.

Remodeling a store that can't close is a different kind of problem. It requires a contractor who understands retail operations — not just construction. RJB has been delivering open-store remodels for America's largest retail chains for over two decades, and we've built our processes around one guiding principle: the customer experience comes first.

Our crews work nights, weekends, and holiday shutdowns to keep construction off the sales floor. We phase the work to maximize selling space throughout the remodel, protect merchandise and existing fixtures during construction, and ensure the store is fully operational every single morning before doors open.

From a single department refresh to a complete store renovation, RJB manages the full scope — flooring, ceilings, walls, fixtures, signage, and equipment — in a coordinated sequence that respects your schedule and your customers.

What's Included

Remodels built around your hours.

01
Phased Construction
We sequence your remodel in phases that keep the largest possible selling floor open while work progresses behind barriers. Customers shop; crews build — in parallel, every night.
02
Overnight Work
RJB crews mobilize after close and are out before opening — every time. No surprises for your store manager, no dust on the shelves, and no construction mess greeting your first customers of the day.
03
Department Resets
Fixture moves, merchandise protection, and department rebuilds handled by crews who understand live retail environments. We work around your planogram, your product, and your people.
04
Full-Store Renovation
When the store needs a full gut and refresh, we bring the complete trade package and manage the closedown-to-reopening timeline. RJB coordinates every scope so you reopen on time and on brand.

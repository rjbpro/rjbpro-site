Asset Maintenance

Brand-standard upkeep between capital cycles.

Capital projects get the attention. The work between them — the coatings programs, envelope repairs, and brand-standard upkeep that keep your portfolio looking and performing the way it should — often doesn't. RJB built its business on exactly this work, starting as a commercial coatings specialist in 1992 and never losing that precision.

Brand standards don't just apply to new construction. They apply to every store, every day. A faded exterior, a failing caulk joint, or peeling interior paint is a customer experience problem as much as a facilities problem. RJB's asset maintenance programs are designed to prevent those failures — not just respond to them.

We manage coatings programs, building envelope maintenance, and brand-standard upkeep for national retail portfolios. Whether it's a single-location refresh or a 500-store managed program, our approach is the same: show up, do it right, and leave the building in better shape than we found it.

Commercial Coatings

We've been a family-owned company since 1992, and that shapes more than how we run projects. It shapes how we show up off the clock, too. Building things is what we do for a living. Building up the communities around us is part of the same instinct.

## Hands on, not just checks written

Our team has partnered with Habitat for Humanity on volunteer build days, trading the commercial job site for a single-family home and the same tools we use every day. It's work our teams know how to do, put to a different purpose.

## Why it matters to us

The trades gave a lot of our people a career and a path. Showing up for the community is one way we pay that forward, and it's a reminder that the skills we bring to national retail programs are just as valuable a few miles from home.

We'll keep finding ways to put our people, our trucks, and our know-how to work where it counts.

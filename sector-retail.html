Sector: Retail

Where brand meets build.

Retail construction is where customer experience is literally constructed. RJB has spent 30 years building the spaces that define how America shops.

Who We Serve

Retail is our origin, and our largest discipline.

RJB was built on retail. The brands that called us in for a single-store remodel in the 1990s are now handing us 50-unit national programs. That trust was earned project by project, with on-schedule delivery, consistent quality, and a team that communicates like a true partner.

We work across every format of brick-and-mortar retail: big box, specialty, inline mall, strip center, endcap, and freestanding. We've completed full store build-outs, department resets, fixture rollouts, and complete brand reimages. If it's retail construction, we've done it, and we've done it at scale.

Our retail clients include national chains managing multi-year programs and regional operators working through a handful of locations. The level of service is the same. The accountability is the same. The quality standard never changes.

What We Build

Every format, every phase of the lifecycle.

01
Store Build-Outs
Second-generation build-outs for retail locations in any format. We manage the full construction scope with one point of contact and zero surprises at occupancy.
02
Store Remodels
Full interior remodels and department-level rebuilds executed in live stores. We've phased remodels around holiday traffic, inventory events, and operational blackout windows.
03
Fixture & Shelving Rollouts
National fixture replacement and shelving reset programs executed across large portfolios, managed by RJB for faster delivery and tighter quality control.
04
Brand Reimage Programs
Comprehensive brand refresh programs (interior and exterior) that bring existing locations up to a brand's current prototype standard without a full rebuild.
05
Tenant Improvements
Turnkey tenant improvement scopes that take a shell space to a fully operational retail location. We work directly with landlords, brand teams, and local authorities.
06
Ongoing Maintenance Programs
Scheduled and reactive maintenance for retail portfolios that need a reliable partner for recurring repairs, touch-ups, and capital refresh work.
What Makes Retail Hard

Challenges we've already solved.

Live-Store Construction

Most retail remodels happen while the store is open. We've built the scheduling, containment, and communication systems that let construction happen without telling customers there's a problem.

Brand Standard Precision

Retail brands have exacting standards for color, material, finish, and detail. Our project managers read prototype packages fluently and our teams execute them without deviation.

Blackout Windows

Holiday seasons, inventory events, and local blackout windows require precise scheduling. We build the project timeline around your operational calendar, not around what's convenient for the crew.

Portfolio Scale

Managing a 30-unit program isn't 30 times harder than managing one unit, unless you don't have the systems. We do. Same crews, same reporting, same accountability at any volume.

Interior Fit-Outs.

Interior Fit-Outs

From shell to finished, on schedule.

RJB delivers complete interior fit-outs for commercial spaces at every stage, from raw shell conditions in new construction to occupied renovations that need to stay open. We bring the trades, the oversight, and the accountability to take a space from demo to grand opening without the coordination chaos that comes with managing multiple contractors.

Whether you're building out a first location or executing across a national portfolio, RJB scales to meet the program. We've delivered fit-outs in grocery, quick-service, retail, and healthcare environments across 48 states, and we know what it takes to hit an opening date.

What's Included

Full-scope interior delivery.

01
Flooring & Finishes
Hardwood, LVT, polished concrete, and specialty finishes, installed to brand spec and ready for opening day. We coordinate surface prep, transitions, and protection through the full construction sequence.
02
Building Systems
Mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire protection, and lighting upgrades coordinated alongside architectural finishes to ensure every system works together and projects stay on schedule.
03
Millwork & Fixtures
Custom and prefab millwork, fixture installs, and casework coordination handled by our carpentry teams. We manage shop drawings, delivery sequencing, and installation to brand standard.
04
Trade Coordination
One RJB point of contact manages every trade on the job, from demo through punch list. We own the schedule and the accountability, so the trades stay in sequence and nothing slips between subs.

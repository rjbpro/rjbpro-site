Industrial Construction

Built around your operation

Warehouse & Industrial

Build-outs and renovation, planned around live operations

Warehouse and industrial construction moves at the speed of your supply chain, which means delays aren't just inconvenient, they're expensive. RJB brings the same scheduling discipline and operational capability that made us trusted in retail to industrial environments where operational continuity is non-negotiable.

From distribution center build-outs to live-operation renovations in facilities that run 24 hours a day, RJB plans every project around your production schedule. We sequence work to minimize disruption, coordinate with your operations team at every phase, and staff projects with crews experienced in live industrial environments.

Our industrial construction practice includes second-generation build-outs, facility renovations, distribution center fit-outs, and ongoing industrial maintenance programs. Whatever the scope, we show up accountable and stay that way through final punch.

What We Deliver

Industrial construction, built to perform

01
Industrial Build-Outs
Warehouse and industrial build-outs from shell to finished and operational. RJB coordinates all core interior trades directly with structural and MEP subs to deliver on schedule and on budget.
02
Live-Operation Renovation
Renovating an active warehouse without interrupting operations requires a level of planning that most contractors can't provide. RJB sequences work around your operational windows and safety protocols so your facility keeps moving.
03
Distribution Center Fit-Out
Interior build-outs for distribution and fulfillment centers, from office and break room finishes to mezzanines, dock improvements, and warehouse-floor coatings. Delivered on the fast timelines that e-commerce demands.
04
Industrial Maintenance
Ongoing facility maintenance programs that protect your industrial assets and keep them performing at brand standard. Planned preventive maintenance and rapid-response repairs, managed by one accountable team.
